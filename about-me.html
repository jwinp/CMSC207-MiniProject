HOBBIES & INTERESTS GALLERY

Reading Album

One of my longtime hobbies is reading. Reading has always been a great way for me to relax, explore imaginative worlds, and stay inspired. Back in high school, I enjoyed diving into fiction and fantasy novels, especially series like Percy Jackson & the Olympians and The Heroes of Olympus. As time passed, my interest in reading evolved toward manhwas and mangas, which I continue to enjoy today.
